What is an Ephemeral experience?

We aim to bring an experience like no other by walking both tattoo hopefuls and veterans through the entire tattoo process from start to finish.

We've partnered with hundreds of tattoo parlors and artists including famous artists -- Jon Boy and Dr. Woo! Like many artists, they've dealt with an outdated system of emailing and calling their clients. This is painful, costly, and very ineffective.

Our experience consists of 6 steps:
1. Explore the portfolios of hundreds of artists
2. Pick a design from the portfolio or draw it yourself
3. Message an artist you see fit for your needs
4. Make an appointment with the artist
5. Get tatt'ed! Pay through our platform after you're done
6. Write a review of your experience to share with the community
